HOW TO PLAY

Instructions:

* It uses mouse control and it has three stages (levels).

* The main objective of this game is player will move horizontally.

* Obstacles (like dinosaurs, reptiles) will move in vertical direction randomly.

* if the player touches the obstacle he can use the lifes and resume the game, if the lifes are over then the game starts from the begininng.

* Else if the player moves without touching any obstacles he/she will get the score by crossing the each obstacle at last he will reach to his house (Goal) with in the time and he will head to the next level.

* In the second stage the obstacles moving speed will be increased and time will be decreased so that accordingly player have to play if he reaches to his home fastly then he will head to the next stage.

* Third stage is harder than the second stage. In this stage also if the player moves without touching the obstacles he will reaches to his home and the game ends here and score board will gets display.
